Frisian Landscapes is a print-and-play expansion that works seamlessly with any other existing expansion of Uwe Rosenberg's board game Fields of Arle.
Frisian Landscapes is designed by Enrico Mazzardi and illustrated by Dennis Lohausen with the permission of the original publisher Feuerland Spiele. This online version, made by the expansion designer Enrico Mazzardi, is free to use and share, but cannot be used for commercial purposes.
